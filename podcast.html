Friends of Banana Q

Langga Speaks Podcast

Langga' [lang.gâ.] means 'Beloved' in Ilonggo/Hiligaynon, one of the gazillion Filipino dialects. Raising a family as Filipino expats in Dubai is not as simple as it may seem. Join show hosts, Apple and Jeric Defante, for downright honest conversations and full-packed laugh marathons as they share about family, faith and everything in between!

Gleeful Talk Show

Sharing zesty stories to cultivate the happiness and hero within. Guests from all over the world impart their personal experiences, opinions or expertise to help us lead a gleeful life. From developing healthy habits and mindset, to pursuing personal goals, or understanding ourselves and relationships better, you'll definitely have something to learn AND laugh at each episode.

That Broke Pinay Podcast

Ang financial podcast ng babaeng gastadora at walang kaalam-alam sa pagmamanage ng pera! This is for every Filipino na ayaw umamin na gastador sila!

Lagim Podcast

Lagim Podcast brings you stories of crimes from the Philippines. Lagim is a Filipino word that means dread/terror. Every two weeks Filipino-German host, Christine Abrigana, takes a close look at infamous and not so known cases that have certainly given Filipinos that feeling of fear and dread. If you need a true crime fix Filipino-style, then please follow, subscribe and listen to Lagim.

Pop Your Cultural Cherry

From making friends in Europe to seeing snow for the first time; The Pop Your Cultural Cherry podcast aims to give listeners a unique perspective on life in Europe with an Asian/Filipino twist.

Empowered Talks With Roma

Empowering people, one podcast episode at a time. #empoweredtalks with Roma Miclat

Laong-Laan Podcast

I am a work in progress. Just like you. I've been struggling mentally, financially, etc. Just like you. This podcast cannot give you quick remedies to issues about living. But this podcast will help each one of us journey in becoming the better version of ourselves. Let's face our true identity, who we really are, our true selves, not the one that we filter, our true voice, not the one that is influenced by the society we live in.

Deep Podcast

Have you been treading shallow waters recently? Welcome to DEEP. Where we invite you to take the plunge and dive deeper into a daily encounter with God's Living Word. Journey with the collection of personal reflections of other souls as we all draw nearer, and DEEPer to God's heart.

Liberating Me Podcast

Frances G is here to share the juiciest learning curve in her life in the areas of Love, Sex, Relationship & Personal development. A Canada-based Pinay gets candid on her journey towards liberation, decolonization, and stripping down a ton of conditioning that does not serve her.

TrainStation Podcast

Our Own Opinion Podcast

We are a Vancouver based Filipino podcasters. Listen to us being hilarious, weird & serious on a wide variety of topics. Feel the nostalgia, relate with our stories, understand the people & learn from the topics. Be a part of the Filipino pride. Let us connect Pinoy’s from all around the world.
